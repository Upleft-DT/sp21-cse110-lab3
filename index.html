Today I cooked spaghetti with ketchup instead of spaghetti sauce. This is known as Napolitan. Here's a tiny recipe:

  • Ingredients
    • Spaghetti (220 grams)
    • Garlic (1 clove)
    • 1/2 Onion
    • Ketchup (2 Tablespoons)
    • Hot Dogs (Preference)
  • Steps
    1. Put Spaghetti in boiling pot, cook for 10 minutes
    2. 6 minutes in cook Garlic in Olive Oil until fragrant
    3. Add onions, cook until half cooked
    4. Add the hotdog
    5. Add the spaghetti and ketchup then mix
